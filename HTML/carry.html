Carry-On

A young TSA agent fights to outsmart a mysterious traveler who blackmails him into letting a dangerous package slip through security and onto a Christmas Day flight. It is a race against time in a crowded airport where every second counts.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Frequent sequences of intense action, physical altercations, and peril involving a terrorist threat.

Language: Moderate use of strong language and suspense-driven profanity.

Sex: None; focused entirely on the thriller plot.

Substances: Minimal; standard for a thriller setting.

Intensity: High tension and psychological pressure throughout as the protagonist is blackmailed.

Themes: Responsibility, bravery under pressure, and airport security.

Verdict: Rated PG-13 for violence and intense action; suitable for teenagers and adults.
