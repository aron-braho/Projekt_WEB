The Guilty

A demoted police officer assigned to a 911 call center desk receives a harrowing phone call from a kidnapped woman. Working within the confines of the dispatch room, he must navigate a web of terrifying revelations to save her, only to discover that nothing is as it seems and facing the truth is the only way out.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Intense verbal descriptions of violence and blood; scenes of emotional distress; no graphic on-screen physical violence.

Language: Frequent strong profanity throughout the film.

Sex: No sexual content or nudity.

Substances: Character is seen using an asthma inhaler during moments of extreme stress; no drug use.

Intensity: Extremely high psychological tension and claustrophobic atmosphere; themes of mental health crisis and child endangerment.

Themes: Redemption, guilt, systemic pressure, and the weight of past mistakes.

Messages: The importance of listening and the danger of making assumptions under pressure.

Verdict: Rated R for language; recommended for mature audiences due to intense subject matter.
