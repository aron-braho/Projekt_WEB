The Midnight Club (2022)

Inside a hospice for terminally ill teens, eight members of the Midnight Club meet each night at midnight to tell sinister stories – and to look for signs of the supernatural from the beyond. They make a chilling pact: the first to die must send a sign from beyond the grave.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Stylized horror violence and significant jump scares. Includes scenes of self-harm, medical procedures, and blood.

Language: Strong language is used throughout the series, including the "F-word."

Sex: Limited sexual content; some romantic kissing and mature discussions about relationships.

Substances: Characters are shown using various medications for their illnesses; some social drinking and smoking.

Intensity: Very high emotional weight due to the focus on terminal illness, death, and grieving.

Verdict: Rated TV-MA. Best suited for audiences 16+ due to mature themes and horror elements.
