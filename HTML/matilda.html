Matilda

Matilda is an extraordinary girl who discovers she has telekinetic powers. In a world of mean-spirited grown-ups and a school ruled by the terrifying principal Miss Trunchbull, Matilda uses her "secret weapon" to stand tall and help her friends.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Comedic and stylized; characters like Miss Trunchbull throw children or force them into difficult punishments like the "cookie" cake challenge.

Intensity: High tension during scenes with the principal; Matilda's parents are neglectful and rude.

Verdict: Rated PG; a classic story from the author of Willy Wonka.
