Home Alone 2: Lost in New York

Kevin McCallister is back, and this time he's in the Big Apple! After accidentally boarding a flight to New York City while his family flies to Florida, Kevin manages to check himself into the Plaza Hotel. However, his solo vacation is cut short when he crosses paths with his old enemies, the Wet Bandits, who have escaped from prison. Kevin must once again use his wits and a series of elaborate booby traps to defend himself and a local toy store from the bumbling crooks.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Extensive slapstick cartoon violence; characters endure falls, electrocution, and being hit by bricks with no lasting injury.

Language: Very mild; occasional insults like "dummy" or "idiot."

Sex: None; one brief scene where a character is mistakenly thought to be in a romantic embrace.

Substances: Some adult characters seen drinking in the background of a party; no drug use.

Intensity: Some scenes involving being lost in a big city or being chased by criminals may be slightly scary for very young children.

Themes: Family, forgiveness, resourcefulness, and the spirit of Christmas.

Messages: Importance of family and helping those in need, even when you're afraid.

Verdict: Recommended for ages 6+; a classic family-friendly holiday comedy.
