Stranger Things

When a young boy vanishes, a small town uncovers a mystery involving secret experiments, terrifying supernatural forces, and one strange little girl. Set in the 1980s, a group of friends must face the "Upside Down" to save their town.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Includes sci-fi violence, supernatural creatures, and some blood. Characters are often in peril or fighting monsters.

Intensity: High tension and horror elements. Some scenes may be very frightening for younger viewers due to the "Upside Down" atmosphere.

Verdict: Rated TV-14; ideal for teens and fans of 80s nostalgia and sci-fi horror.
