My Spy

A hardened CIA operative finds himself at the mercy of a precocious 9-year-old girl, having been sent undercover to surveil her family. In exchange for not blowing his cover, JJ agrees to teach Sophie how to be a spy, leading to an unexpected bond.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Action-style violence, including explosions, shootings, and hand-to-hand combat, but mostly presented with a comedic tone.

Language: Some moderate profanity and rude humor.

Sex: Brief suggestive references; no nudity.

Substances: Some scenes showing adults drinking alcohol in social settings.

Intensity: High-stakes action sequences that might be intense for younger children.

Themes: Friendship, family, redemption, and mentorship.

Verdict: Rated PG-13; suitable for teens and older children with parental guidance.
