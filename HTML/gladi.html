Gladiator II

Years after witnessing the death of the revered hero Maximus, Lucius is forced to enter the Colosseum after his home is conquered by the tyrannical Emperors of Rome. With rage as his gift, he must fight to return the glory of Rome to its people and fulfill the dream of a free nation.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Intense and brutal gladiatorial combat, large-scale Roman warfare, and stylized action involving weapons and animals.

Intensity: High emotional stakes centered on revenge, survival, and the political corruption of the Roman Empire.

Verdict: Rated R for strong bloody violence; recommended for mature audiences and fans of historical action epics.
