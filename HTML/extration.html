Extraction 2

After barely surviving his previous mission in Dhaka, Bangladesh, Tyler Rake is back, and his team is ready to take on their next mission. Tasked with extracting a family who is at the mercy of a Georgian gangster, Tyler infiltrates one of the world's deadliest prisons in order to save them.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Extreme and relentless. Features a famous 21-minute "oner" action sequence. Includes heavy gunfire, stabbings, bone-breaking combat, and explosions.

Intensity: Very high. The film deals with themes of domestic abuse, prison riots, and high-stakes survival.

Verdict: Rated R for strong bloody violence throughout and language. Recommended for mature action fans.
