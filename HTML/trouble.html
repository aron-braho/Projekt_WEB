Trouble

Trouble is a pampered dog who has lived a life of luxury until his wealthy owner passes away. Suddenly found wandering the streets of the big city, he must learn to escape his owner's greedy children and survive the "real world." Along the way, he teams up with a group of street-smart animals and an aspiring singer to find the true meaning of home.
