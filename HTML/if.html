IF (Imaginary Friends)

A young girl who goes through a difficult experience begins to see everyone's imaginary friends who have been left behind as their real-life friends grew up. She embarks on a magical adventure to reconnect forgotten "IFs" with their kids.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Very mild; mostly slapstick humor and animated characters falling.

Language: Very clean; family-friendly dialogue.

Sex: None.

Substances: None.

Intensity: Emotional themes regarding growing up and loss might be touching for children and adults alike.

Themes: Imagination, friendship, childhood nostalgia, and healing.

Verdict: Rated PG; a heartwarming movie for the whole family.
