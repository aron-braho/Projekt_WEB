Family Switch

Jess and Bill Walker are doing their best to keep their family connected as their children grow older, more independent, and more distant. When a chance encounter with an astrological reader causes the family to wake up in a full-body switch on the morning of the most important day of each of their lives, the Walkers must unite to land a promotion, college interview, record deal, and soccer tryout.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Minor slapstick comedy and sports-related collisions.

Language: Very mild; appropriate for a general family audience.

Sex: Brief comedic moments regarding awkward body-swap situations; no nudity.

Substances: No drug use; minimal social drinking in background settings.

Intensity: Lighthearted and frantic energy; very low stress levels.

Themes: Family bonding, empathy, and understanding different perspectives.

Messages: Walking a mile in someone else's shoes can change your entire relationship with them.

Verdict: Rated PG; perfect for a family movie night with all ages.
