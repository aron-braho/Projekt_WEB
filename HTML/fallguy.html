The Fall Guy

Colt Seavers is a stuntman who left the business a year earlier to focus on both his physical and mental health. He's drafted back into service when the star of a high-budget studio movie, directed by his ex, goes missing. While the mystery deepens, Colt performs the most dangerous stunts of his life to save the film and get the girl back.

Parents' Guide

Violence: High-octane stunts, stylized fights, and explosions; very little gore.

Language: Frequent mild to moderate profanity.

Sex: Romantic flirting and some kissing; no explicit scenes.

Substances: Social drinking throughout.

Intensity: Fast-paced action sequences and suspenseful stunts.

Themes: Professionalism, redemption, and the unsung heroes of filmmaking.

Messages: Success isn't just about the spotlight; it's about the hard work behind the scenes.

Verdict: Rated PG-13 for action and language; suitable for teenagers and adults.
