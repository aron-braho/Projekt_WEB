Mary Poppins Returns

Now an adult with three children, Jane and Michael Banks receive a visit from the enigmatic Mary Poppins following a personal loss. Through her unique magical skills, and with the aid of her friend Jack, she helps the family rediscover the joy and wonder missing from their lives.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Very mild; a brief scene involving a chase in an animated sequence.

Language: Completely clean; suitable for all ages.

Sex: None; only lighthearted singing and dancing.

Substances: No drug or alcohol use.

Intensity: Some emotional moments regarding the loss of a parent and the threat of losing a home.

Themes: Hope, imagination, family bonds, and childhood wonder.

Verdict: Rated G; a perfect musical experience for the entire family.
