The Weekend Away

A weekend getaway to Croatia goes awry when a woman is accused of killing her best friend. As she attempts to clear her name and uncover the truth, her efforts unearth a painful secret. With the local police suspicious and her memories of the night blurry, she must piece together exactly what happened before she becomes the next victim of a web of lies.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Brief but intense scenes of physical struggle; depiction of a dead body (non-graphic); themes of murder and kidnapping.

Language: Moderate use of profanity throughout the film.

Sex: Suggestive dialogue; scenes in a club environment; references to infidelity and betrayal.

Substances: Significant focus on social drinking, clubbing, and a plot point involving the possible drugging of characters.

Intensity: High psychological tension; themes of gaslighting and being alone in a foreign country.

Themes: Trust, betrayal, memory loss, and the dark side of close friendships.

Messages: Be careful who you trust and look beneath the surface of appearances.

Verdict: Rated TV-14/PG-13; suitable for older teens and adults due to mature themes.
