Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery

Five long-time friends are invited to the Greek island home of billionaire Miles Bron. All five know Bron from way back and owe their current wealth, fame, and careers to him. The main event is a murder-weekend game with Bron to be the victim. In reality, they all have reasons to kill him. Also invited is Benoit Blanc, the world's greatest detective.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Intense action and stylized fights; some blood, no graphic injuries.

Language: Occasional mild profanity (e.g., "damn").

Sex: Brief romantic kissing; no nudity.

Substances: Social drinking; no drug use.

Intensity: Menacing atmosphere and dark settings in some scenes.

Themes: Friendship, betrayal, and bravery.

Messages: Focuses on teamwork and standing up for the truth.

Verdict: Recommended for ages 12+ due to mystery complexity and intensity.
