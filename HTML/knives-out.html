Knives Out (2019)

When renowned crime novelist Harlan Thrombey is found dead at his estate just after his 85th birthday, the inquisitive and debonair Detective Benoit Blanc is mysteriously enlisted to investigate. From Harlan's dysfunctional family to his devoted staff, Blanc sifts through a web of red herrings and self-serving lies to uncover the truth.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Brief images of a bloody throat wound; some moderate action and verbal threats.

Language: Strong language throughout, including one "F-bomb" and several other profanities.

Sex: No nudity or sex scenes; very mild suggestive references.

Substances: Characters are seen drinking alcohol (champagne, wine, beer) and some characters are shown under the influence of medication.

Intensity: Suspenseful mystery atmosphere, but generally lighthearted and comedic in tone.

Verdict: Rated PG-13. Generally safe for teenagers and up.
