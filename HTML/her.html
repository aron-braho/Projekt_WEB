Clifford the Big Red Dog

When middle-schooler Emily Elizabeth meets a magical animal rescuer who gifts her a little, red puppy, she never anticipated waking up to find a giant ten-foot hound in her small New York City apartment. Emily and her fun but impulsive uncle Casey set out on an adventure that will take a bite out of the Big Apple.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Mild slapstick action; a few scenes of animals in peril and some cartoonish chases.

Language: Very clean; family-appropriate language throughout.

Sex: None.

Substances: No drug or alcohol use shown.

Intensity: Low; some tense moments when a greedy scientist tries to capture Clifford.

Themes: Unconditional love, acceptance, and standing up for what is right.

Verdict: Rated PG; a charming and fun movie for kids and families.
