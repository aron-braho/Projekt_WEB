Lift

An international heist crew, led by Cyrus Whitaker, is recruited to steal $500 million in gold from a passenger plane at 40,000 feet. The catch? They have to do it mid-flight to prevent the fortune from ending up in the hands of a dangerous terrorist group. It's a race against time and gravity in this high-stakes action comedy.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Frequent action violence including shootouts, explosions, and hand-to-hand combat; generally bloodless.

Language: Moderate profanity throughout.

Sex: Brief suggestive dialogue and some flirting; no explicit scenes.

Substances: Social drinking in high-end settings.

Intensity: High-altitude tension and fast-paced heist sequences.

Themes: Teamwork, redemption, and high-tech crime.

Messages: Trusting your team is the only way to pull off the impossible.

Verdict: Rated PG-13 for violence and language; best for teens and up.
