Rebel Ridge

A former Marine confronts corruption in a small town when local law enforcement unjustly seizes the bag of cash he needs to post his cousin's bail. This leads to a high-stakes standoff where he must use his specialized skills to take down the department.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Intense sequences of realistic violence, hand-to-hand combat, and tactical weaponry use.

Language: Frequent strong language and profanity throughout the film.

Sex: None.

Substances: Some references to systemic drug issues and minor alcohol use.

Intensity: Very high tension and suspenseful scenes involving police confrontation.

Themes: Justice, systemic corruption, morality, and civil rights.

Verdict: Rated TV-MA/R; recommended for mature audiences and fans of grounded thrillers.
