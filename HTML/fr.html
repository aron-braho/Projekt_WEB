Fractured (2019)

Driving cross-country, Ray, his wife and daughter stop at a highway rest area where his daughter falls and breaks her arm. After a frantic rush to the hospital and a clash with the check-in nurse, Ray is finally able to get her to a doctor. While the wife and daughter go downstairs for an MRI, Ray, exhausted, passes out in a chair in the lobby. Upon waking up, they have no record of Ray's family ever being checked in.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Moderate. Includes scenes of accidents, hospital tension, and some physical altercations. Some disturbing imagery related to medical procedures.

Language: Occasional strong profanity.

Sex: None; focused entirely on the mystery and family dynamic.

Substances: References to past addiction and social drinking.

Intensity: Very high psychological tension. The movie deals with themes of gaslighting, memory loss, and mental instability.

Verdict: Rated TV-MA. Best suited for adult audiences due to psychological intensity.
