Bean: The Ultimate Disaster Movie

Mr. Bean, a bumbling security guard at the Royal National Gallery in London, is sent to Los Angeles to represent the gallery at the unveiling of the priceless painting "Whistler's Mother." As he stays with a museum curator and his family, Bean's unique brand of chaos leads to the destruction of the painting and nearly ruins everyone's lives, only to be saved by his own peculiar logic.

Parents' Guide

Violence: Slapstick comedy violence; some mild peril and accidents.

Language: Very little dialogue; some mild insults.

Sex: Brief comedic situations; no nudity.

Substances: Social situations with mild alcohol use.

Intensity: High-energy physical comedy and awkward social situations.

Themes: Chaos, friendship, and unintended consequences.

Messages: Even the most bumbling individual can find a way to fix their mistakes.

Verdict: Recommended for all ages, though rated PG-13 for some "risque humor."
